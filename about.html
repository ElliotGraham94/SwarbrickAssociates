ABOUT

Swarbrick Associates are a well established architectural practice based in Carlisle, led by principle David Swarbrick.

Our work covers a wide range of building types and scales. Recent and current projects include education, housing, offices, cultural, community, mixed-use regeneration, masterplanning and urban design. All provide high quality and sustainable environments for their users, offer good value for money within agreed budgets and make a positive contribution to their varying contexts.

We are experienced in working closely with our clients, and welcome that close dialogue between Architect and Client which is such an important part of the best design solutions.

Established in 1997 as an architectural practice, we maintain a rigorous approach developed through years of experience working alongside clients with high aspirations, often on difficult sites, strict programme deadlines and budgets.

Success has been achieved by gaining a thorough understanding of client needs, detailed knowledge of the building type, disciplined and intellectual analysis of the context, and by producing imaginative responses and creative solutions. The result is design and construction of high quality, delivered within agreed budgets and programmes for projects throughout the country.
